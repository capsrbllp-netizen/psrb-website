Our Services

Six integrated service domains, each led by a dedicated partner — delivering specialist depth with the convenience of one trusted firm.

📋

Audit & Assurance

Our audit practice is built on a foundation of independence, technical rigour, and deep sector understanding. Every engagement is partner-led — ensuring quality, consistency, and accountability throughout.

Reliability, independence, and regulatory confidence — delivering audits that stakeholders, regulators, and lenders can depend on.
  • Statutory Audit (Companies Act 2013)
  • Tax Audit (u/s 44AB of Income Tax Act)
  • Internal Audit & Process Review
  • Concurrent Audit
  • Bank Audit & Stock Audit
  • Limited Review & Due Diligence
  • Compliance Audit
📜

Taxation & Regulatory Services

Taxation is our core — handled by experts with Big-4 exposure and deep experience in both GST and direct tax. We move beyond mere compliance to deliver structured, proactive tax planning that protects and optimises.

Accuracy, risk mitigation, and timely compliance — keeping you ahead of regulatory changes, not behind them.
  • Income Tax Advisory & Return Filing
  • Corporate & Non-Corporate Tax Compliance
  • GST Registration, Returns & Audits
  • Representation before Tax Authorities & Tribunals
  • Tax Structuring & Litigation Support
  • Corporate & International Tax Advisory
  • Cross-Border Structuring & India Entry/Exit Planning
📊

Valuation & Transaction Advisory

Led by an IBBI Registered Valuer for financial securities, our valuation practice delivers credible, court-ready, and regulatory-compliant reports. We apply internationally recognised methodologies — DCF, Comparable Company Analysis, and NAV approaches.

Defensible valuation backed by sound methodology, IBBI compliance, and transparent workings that withstand regulatory and judicial scrutiny.
  • Business Valuation
  • Financial Asset & Securities Valuation (IBBI RV)
  • Valuation for Banking & Financing Transactions
  • Valuation for Mergers & Acquisitions
  • Regulatory & Compliance Valuation (FEMA, Companies Act)
  • Fairness Opinions
  • Financial Modelling & Projections
🏦

Project Finance & Banking Support

We bridge the gap between businesses and lenders — preparing the documentation, financial projections, and liaison support that gives your project the best chance of securing funding at optimal terms.

Structuring bankable proposals that align lender requirements with business realities — ensuring timely, sustainable funding.
  • Project Reports & Detailed Project Report (DPR) Preparation
  • CMA Data & Financial Projections
  • Term Loan & Working Capital Assistance
  • Bank / NBFC Liaison & Negotiations
  • Loan Restructuring & Enhancement Proposals
  • Documentation & Compliance Support
  • Government Scheme Advisory (CGTMSE, PMEGP etc.)
⚙️

Process Automation & IFC

Built on hands-on manufacturing industry experience, our process advisory goes beyond policy documents — we design and implement controls that actually work on the ground, are audit-ready, and scale with your business.

Building strong internal systems that reduce leakage, improve transparency, and enable scalable, audit-confident operations.
  • Internal Financial Control (IFC) Design & Review
  • Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) Development
  • Accounting Process Automation
  • ERP Selection, Implementation Advisory & MIS Design
  • Process Risk Mapping & Control Assessment
🚀

Startup & MSME Advisory

Startups and MSMEs face unique challenges — undercapitalisation, compliance gaps, and lack of institutional-grade financial infrastructure. We specialise in bridging this gap: from day-one structuring to investor-ready financials.

Giving early-stage and growth businesses the financial backbone that attracts investors, satisfies lenders, and enables confident scaling.
  • Business Structuring & Entity Selection Advisory
  • Entity Formation & Registration (Company, LLP, etc.)
  • Accounting & Compliance Setup
  • Growth Advisory & Financial Planning
  • Investor & Lender Readiness Preparation
  • MSME Registration & Scheme Benefits Advisory
